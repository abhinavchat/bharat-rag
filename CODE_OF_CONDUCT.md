# Code of Conduct

This project adopts the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/) Code of Conduct.

In short:

- Be respectful and inclusive.
- Do not harass or discriminate against others.
- Assume good intent but be open to feedback.

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behaviour
may be reported by opening an issue or contacting the maintainers privately.
